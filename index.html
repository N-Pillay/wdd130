About Me

Niven Pillay

(Niv)

My name is Niven Pillay. I was born in Johannesburg, South Africa. However, I’ve recently moved to the United Kingdom. Currently, I live in Manchester. Living alone in a new country can have many challenges, but I’m optimistic about the future.

I have a small family, mainly keeping in contact with my mom and dad. I am not married. I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I was baptised in 2015, and the hymn I’d chosen for my baptism was “High on a Mountain Top”. My favourite scripture is Doctrine and Covenants 6:36, “Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not.”.

I studied at the University of Pretoria and earned a diploma in veterinary nursing. I’m working in the UK as a registered veterinary nurse. My goal would be to attain a bachelor's degree from BYU-I, hoping to use my academic credentials to apply for veterinary medicine and finally achieve my dream of becoming a full-fledged veterinarian.

I am currently enrolled in the BS Applied Technology degree course. The web fundamentals course would be a stepping stone toward designing websites. With the added courses from my degree, I would be learning both front and back-end development. These skills, although unrelated to my field of work, add dynamics and alternate forms of thinking which I feel would be beneficial in the field of veterinary medicine.
